Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-07. This policy describes how Tvara handles data when you use the website and games.

TL;DR: Tvara works without an account. Your scores and streak can be stored on your device using localStorage. We also use Google Analytics to understand traffic and improve the experience.

What data is stored on your device

By default, Tvara stores a small profile (a generated username, streak, and best session score) and per-game best scores in your browser using localStorage. This data stays on your device and can be cleared by clearing site data in your browser.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to measure site usage (for example: page views, device type, approximate location, and interactions). Google may set cookies or use similar identifiers for analytics.

Leaderboards and accounts

Today, public leaderboards are simulated and your profile is local-only. If we introduce optional sign-in and server-stored leaderboards in the future, we will update this policy with what we store, why we store it, and how you can delete it.

Data retention

Local data persists until you clear it. Analytics data retention is controlled in Google Analytics settings.

Children’s privacy

If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: hello@tvaralabs.in

Changes

We may update this policy. The “Effective date” above will reflect the latest version.
