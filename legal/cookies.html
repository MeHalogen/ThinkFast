Cookies

Effective date: 2026-04-07.

Do we use cookies?

Tvara may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics via Google Analytics. Separately, Tvara stores game progress on your device using localStorage (this is not a cookie, but it is local browser storage).

How to control cookies

  • You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings.
  • You can clear site data to remove localStorage data.
